Our
History

Revolution’s story began with a simple idea: the only way to create the highest quality cannabis products in every category was to control the process from seed to shelf, incorporating every aspect of the plant possible.

Our President and lead cultivator, Dusty Shroyer, has spent over 20 years honing a proprietary growing style that enables us to best translate premium flower into other formats, becoming a fully-integrated, single-source operation.

Today, Revolution touches multiple states, but much of our most important work still happens in the cultivation rooms in our Delavan, Illinois facility. Our genetics team hunts over 1500 phenotypes each year where they identify unique strains with desirable terpenes and cannabinoid profiles, making ours the most dedicated genetics research program in the country. This program has produced our proprietary strains that have won 14 total Cannabis Cup awards, including 9 first place wins.

As we have expanded from cultivator to retail operator, we continue to use the most exacting standards in the business, ensuring that quality is maintained without interruption. We operate retail locations in 4 states, aiming to provide a welcoming, educated approach to helping our patients find the right product fit.
