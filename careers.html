Revolutionary

Opportunities that span the cannabis industry and empower people to live their best lives while helping others to do the same.
We are a multi-state cannabis operator with locations in Illinois, Arkansas, Missouri, Maryland and soon to be Florida. Balancing cannabis culture with business, our teams grow and manufacture award winning products and provide exceptional service. We are always looking for people who believe in cannabis and love the cannabis industry. We are seeking people excited about the knowledge and experience to be gained at the forefront of this industry—people who feel joy about joining a Revolution.

PERKS & BENEFITS

Comprehensive medical, dental and vision plans.
